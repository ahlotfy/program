Owl at night

owls hunt mainly at night, tackling prey ranging from mice, frogs, and snakes to rabbits, skunks, crows, and geese.

owl in the daytime

Some owls do not exhibit nocturnal behavior at all. A few species are diurnal, meaning they are active during the day.

Owl food

Most owls eat mainly small rodents, but their diets also include birds, fish, insects, and larger creatures like young deer and foxes.

owl's eyes

An owl's eyes can account for up to three percent of its entire body weight, whereas a human's eyes make up about 0.0003 percent of its body weight.
